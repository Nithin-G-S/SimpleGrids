Who we are

Simple Grids is an energy technology platform that creates a positive impact on the environment and the economy by empowering customers to adopt the renewable technology solutions and gain utility connections at ease.

Use our platform for:

  • Understanding the range of renewable technologies and promotional schemes
  • Selecting pre-designed generic packages or bespoke fit using assessors for your buildings
  • Posting an installation job to receive, compare and accept quotes
  • Availing end to end services from a range of service providers with standardised cost that can take the project to completion
  • Managing the project with statement of works and milestones linked to the payment portal to avoid disputes and controversial snag lists
